# Privacy Policy for Savora

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Savora ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account using Google Sign-In, we collect:
- Your email address
- Your display name
- Your profile picture (if available)

### User-Generated Content
When you use Savora, we store:
- **Pantry Items**: Ingredients you add to your virtual pantry
- **Saved Recipes**: Recipes you save to your collection
- **Recipe Collections**: Custom collections you create
- **Grocery Lists**: Items on your shopping list
- **Preferences**: Your dietary restrictions, cooking skill level, and other settings

### Usage Data
We collect information about how you use the app:
- Features you use (recipe generation, pantry management, etc.)
- Usage limits and quotas (for free/Pro plan management)
- Timestamps of actions (for daily limit resets)

### AI-Generated Content
When you request recipe suggestions or modifications:
- Your requests are sent to OpenAI's API to generate personalized recipes
- Your pantry contents may be included to provide relevant suggestions
- We do not store your conversation history permanently on third-party servers

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Savora app
- Generate personalized recipe recommendations based on your pantry
- Sync your data across devices
- Manage your subscription and usage limits
- Improve our services and develop new features
- Communicate with you about your account or updates

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI
We use OpenAI's API to generate recipes and process your requests. Your prompts and pantry data may be sent to OpenAI for processing. Please review [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/) for more information.

### Google AdMob
For free users, we display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. Pro subscribers do not see ads.

### Supabase
We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. Your data is stored securely on Supabase's servers. Please review [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy) for more information.

### RevenueCat
For subscription management, we use RevenueCat. They process:
- Purchase transactions
- Subscription status
- Anonymous usage analytics

Please review [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) for more information.

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers
- Your password is never stored (authentication is handled by Google)
- We implement access controls to protect your data

## Data Retention

- Your account data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings
- After account deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data
- **Export**: Export your recipes and data
- **Opt-out**: Disable personalized ads through device settings

To exercise these rights, use the options in the app's Settings menu or contact us at vincivisionllc@gmail.com.

## Children's Privacy

Savora is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located. By using Savora, you consent to this transfer.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

**Email**: vincivisionllc@gmail.com
