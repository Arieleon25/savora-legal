  ## Frequently Asked Questions                                                                        
                                                                                                       
  ### Account & Login                                                                                  
                                                                                                       
  **How do I sign in?**                                                                                
  Savora supports Sign in with Apple, Google, and Facebook. Choose your preferred method on the login  
  screen.                                                                                              
                                                                                                       
  **How do I delete my account?**                                                                      
  Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data within 30 days.
                                                                                                       
  ### Pantry & Recipes                                                                                 
                                                                                                       
  **How do I add ingredients to my pantry?**                                                           
  Tap the + button on the Pantry tab, then type or use voice input to add ingredients.                 
                                                                                                       
  **Why don't I see recipe suggestions?**                                                              
  Make sure you have ingredients in your pantry. The AI needs at least a few ingredients to generate   
  recipe ideas.                                                                                        
                                                                                                       
  **Can I save recipes?**                                                                              
  Yes! Tap the heart icon on any recipe to save it to your collection.                                 
                                                                                                       
  ### Subscription                                                                                     
                                                                                                       
  **What's included in Savora Pro?**                                                                   
  Pro subscribers get unlimited recipe generations, no ads, and access to premium features.            
                                                                                                       
  **How do I manage my subscription?**                                                                 
  Your subscription is managed through Apple. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to modify or   
  cancel.                                                                                              
                                                                                                       
  **How do I restore my purchase?**                                                                    
  Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases.                                                   
                                                                                                       
  ### Technical Issues                                                                                 
                                                                                                       
  **The app is crashing. What should I do?**                                                           
  Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, try deleting and reinstalling the app. Your
   data is synced to your account and will be restored when you sign back in.                          
                                                                                                       
  **Recipes aren't loading.**                                                                          
  Check your internet connection. Recipe generation requires an active connection.
